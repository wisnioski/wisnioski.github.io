Dr. Emily Wisnioski

I am currently an ASTRO-3D Fellow hosted at the Australian National University in Canberra, Australia. I study the life-cycle of galaxies at the peak epoch of mass formation. A focus of my research is to make evolutionary connections across cosmic time.

I am always interested in connecting with new undergrad, Masters, and PhD students so please contact me if you are interested in galaxy evolution or in projects I have advertised at ANU.
